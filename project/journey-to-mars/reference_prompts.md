# Reference Image Prompts for Journey to Mars Music Video

## 1. Dawn of Tools (2001 Bone Homage)

**Primary Prompt:**
Early human ancestor standing on rocky outcrop, raising animal bone tool triumphantly to sky, direct homage to 2001: A Space Odyssey monolith scene, dramatic low angle shot, golden hour lighting, epic cinematic composition, vast African savanna background, sense of revelation and discovery, photorealistic style, high contrast, emotional moment of technological birth

**Alternative Prompts:**
- Prehistoric human with bone weapon silhouetted against dramatic sunset, 2001 Space Odyssey inspired, cinematic wide shot, earth tones, sense of destiny
- Close-up of early human hand gripping bone tool, determined expression, shallow depth of field, dramatic lighting, moment of human innovation

## 2. Fire Discovery

**Primary Prompt:**
Prehistoric humans gathered around flickering fire for first time, faces illuminated by warm orange glow, mixture of fear and wonder, dark cave interior, dramatic lighting from flames, smoke rising, sense of power and danger, cinematic wide shot, photorealistic, high contrast between light and shadow

**Alternative Prompts:**
- Close-up of early human hand reaching toward flame, discovery moment, warm lighting on face, expression of awe, shallow depth of field
- Group of early humans huddling around fire at night, protection from darkness, vast wilderness beyond, sense of community and survival

## 3. The Wheel Invention

**Primary Prompt:**
Ancient inventor/artisan workshop, first wooden wheel being completed, dramatic lighting from single window, sense of breakthrough moment, tools and materials scattered around, focused craftsman with wheel, dust motes in light beams, Renaissance painting style meets photorealism, warm earth tones

**Alternative Prompts:**
- First wheel being tested on primitive cart, ancient village setting, observers watching in amazement, sense of progress and innovation
- Close-up of wooden wheel construction, detailed craftsmanship, hand tools, texture of wood, moment of creation

## 4. Agriculture Development

**Primary Prompt:**
Early humans planting first crops in organized rows, transition from hunter-gatherer to farming, fertile valley landscape, golden light of dawn, sense of hope and future, small community working together, primitive tools, emergence of civilization, wide landscape shot

**Alternative Prompts:**
- First harvest celebration, bounty of crops, community gathering, sense of abundance and security, warm golden hour lighting
- Aerial view of early agricultural fields, geometric patterns emerging from landscape, human impact on earth

## 5. Cities Formation
